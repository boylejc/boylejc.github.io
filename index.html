I am John Boyle (he/him), a data and research analyst focusing on the intersection of community development, economic development, and housing policy.


Previously, I worked as the research analyst for the Pittsburgh Community Reinvestment Group (PCRG), an organization dedicated to building capacity for other local community and advocacy groups, and seeking positive racial and economic outcomes through research and advocacy.


For more data analysis outside the major reports I wrote for PCRG, check out my data blog.





Professional Project Portfolio

PCRG's Annual Mortgage Lending Study, examining Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA) data from 2012-2021 in Allegheny County, Pennsylvania.

PCRG's Corporate Housing Study, tracking purchases of single-family housing units by corporate entities in Allegheny County, Pennsylvania.

PCRG's Black Homeownership Report, comparing trends in Black Homeownership in Allegheny County and the Pittsburgh MSA to similar cities/MSAs.

Areas of Interest and Expertise

Census Tract Demographic Analysis

Examining shifts in census tract racial and ethnic demographics and economic conditions.

Community Needs Indexing

Quantifying socio-economic changes to compare across multiple geographies and time periods.

Housing Transaction and Mortgage Data

Analyzing Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA) and real estate transaction data.

Economic Development Indicators

Calculating location quotients, shift share analysis, and other value economic indicators.

Public Transportation Data

Reviewing the National Transit Database to better understand local ridership in public transit.

Community Engagement

Leading working groups, and working on the ground with community leaders, stakeholders, and elected officials.
